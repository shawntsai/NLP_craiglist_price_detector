It has been an absolutely incredible adventure motorcycle, extremely reliable, fast, and fun. Always a grin when riding this beast.

It is the Adventure model, so upgraded crash bars, larger fuel tank, more travel in suspension, among other things.

This bike is truly amazing, it will take you anywhere and at just 42,500 miles, it still has a LONG life ahead! These big oil cooled bikes go 100k plus so easily with good care and maintenance.

The bad:

Back in July I was involved in a minor accident. Was splitting lanes and cars in front slammed on breaks and I did as well, but I ended up hitting the rear right portion of a mustang with my fender, which made me and the bike go down. If you dont mind a little road rash, and want to save thousands on an incredible BMW, this one is perfect for you. This bike is made to go off road, back mountain trails, tipped over, banged up. Thats what its made for. They named this special model "Adventure" for a reason!
